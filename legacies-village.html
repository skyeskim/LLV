Design Detail — Vaulted Architecture

Cathedral
ceilings,
intentional light.

"The space itself teaches. When a child looks up, they should see possibility — not a drop ceiling."

Inspired by the warmth of Highland vernacular interiors, the base floor features 24-foot vaulted ceilings with exposed white oak timber framing. Clerestory windows track morning light from east to west. Every material — stone, wood, woven textile — is chosen to ground children in the natural world.

Floor Three — Business Suites

Small businesses.
Real roots.

Boutique suites on the third floor are purpose-leased to wellness and family-forward businesses. Every tenant is community-vetted. The building itself is the incubator.

Suite A — Wellness

Day Spa & Skin Studio

600–900 sq. ft.

  • Private treatment rooms with operable skylights
  • Plumbing and ventilation for steam + aromatherapy
  • Waiting lounge with direct garden terrace access
  • Below-market lease tied to community membership discount

Suite B — Health

Nutrition & Wellness Clinic

500–700 sq. ft.

  • Consultation rooms with natural light and greenery
  • Shared lab and storage for nutrition practitioners
  • Integrated with kitchen programming downstairs
  • Priority access for mothers enrolled in co-work

Suite C — Creative

Studio & Creative Agency

400–800 sq. ft.

  • Open-plan studio with exposed beam ceilings
  • North-facing light for photography and art
  • Access to shared library and community resources
  • Flexible lease — monthly or annual terms available
